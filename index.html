AI Use Case Explorer

What is This?

This is a simple, interactive guide to help beginners understand and explore how artificial intelligence tools can support everyday life, work, finances, learning, and more. Whether you're brand new to AI or just looking for real-life examples, this site will walk you through practical use cases using tools like ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Replit, Firebase Studio, and others.

How to Get the Most Out of AI — The "Crab Way"

Context: Start with who the AI is helping and why.
Rules: Set limitations or preferred styles.
Action: Clearly state what you want the AI to do.
Behavior: Describe the tone or personality of the answer.

Example prompt:
You are an AI tutor helping a 12-year-old learn about budgeting. Explain how Replit and Firebase Studio work together to make a budgeting app. Keep it under 100 words and make it sound fun and clear.
