AI Use Case Explorer

What is This?

This site is your beginner-friendly launchpad for exploring real-world AI tools that work together to help you get things done faster, smarter, and cheaper — without needing a tech background.

Think of it like a roadmap. It connects powerful apps that are free or low-cost — like ChatGPT for brainstorming, Replit for building small apps, and Firebase Studio for storing data or automating workflows. Instead of just hearing “AI is cool,” this site shows you how to use it — right now — for things you actually care about.

Even if you've never used AI before, this guide is for you.

Real-World Use Case Examples

  • Meal Planner: Use ChatGPT to build your weekly meal plan and grocery list, then send it to a printable format or app via Replit.
  • Workout Scheduler: Ask Gemini to recommend routines and track your workouts in a Firebase dashboard.
  • Invoice Tracker: Create invoices with ChatGPT, build the tracker in Replit, and store data in Firebase Studio.
  • Job Prep Assistant: Use Claude to review your resume, generate cover letters, and simulate interview questions.
  • Study Tool Generator: Upload notes to NotebookLM, generate flashcards with ChatGPT, and quiz yourself daily.
  • Trip Planner: Ask Perplexity to find hidden local spots, create the itinerary in ChatGPT, and turn it into a mobile-friendly site with Framer.

How to Get the Most Out of AI — The "CRAB Method"

Context: Start with who the AI is helping and why.
Rules: Set limitations or preferred styles.
Action: Clearly state what you want the AI to do.
Behavior: Describe the tone or personality of the answer.

Prompt Builder Options

Example prompt:
You are an AI tutor helping a 12-year-old learn about budgeting. Explain how Replit and Firebase Studio work together to make a budgeting app. Keep it under 100 words and make it sound fun and clear.
